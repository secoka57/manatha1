8 Years Experience

Since our establishment in 2017, Coka & Brothers Africa Projects Pty Ltd has been providing professional business registration, company compliance, consulting services, and digital solutions to businesses across South Africa. Our goal is to help companies grow efficiently and legally with expert guidance at every step.

Vision: To be the leading provider of business compliance, consulting, and digital solutions in South Africa, enabling our clients to achieve sustainable growth and success.

Mission: Deliver reliable, personalized, and efficient services that ensure businesses operate legally, remain compliant, and reach their full potential.

Our team specializes in company registration, tax compliance, business profiles, business plans, financial statements, and website development, ensuring a comprehensive solution for your business needs.
